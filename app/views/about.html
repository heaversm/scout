Brian Drucker

Creative Director / Director

Brian approaches every project with pen and paper, and an open mind. He studied Architecture and Civil Engineering, then went to work for Frank Gehry. That architectural foundation differentiates and informs his spatial, sculptural approach to design, 3D and visual effects.

His resumé is just as dimensional, including 3D & VFX projects at Sony Imageworks, Cinesite, Dreamworks, Nickelodeon and Digital Domain. And design and directing at Trollback, Psyop, The Ebeling Group, Loyal Kaspar, Freestyle Collective and most recently, Superfad.



Chace Hartman

Creative Director / Director

Chace works by a process of discovery through experimentation. He began as a technical illustrator before expanding into motion graphics. His approach to creative problem solving is best summed up by the words: What if? This curiosity leads to unexpected design solutions.

His bi-coastal resumé includes Digital Kitchen, Brand New School, Ebeling Group, MK12, Buck, and most
recently, Superfad.
